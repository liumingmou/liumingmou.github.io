I am now a postdoc in the Department of Computer Science with funding from BARC at the University of Copenhagen, hosted by Mikkel Thorup and Rasmus Pagh. Previously, I was a research fellow in the Division of Mathematical Sciences at Nanyang Technological University hosted by Li, Yi(李翼). I did my PhD at Nanjing University, where I was fortunate to have Professor Yin, Yitong(尹一通) as my adviser.

My research interests lie in the intersection of theoretical computer science and database theory, especially data structure complexity and dimensionality reduction.
