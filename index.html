I am a tenure-track Associate Professor at the School of Intelligent Software and Engineering, Nanjing University. Prior to the professership, I was a postdoc in the Department of Computer Science with funding from BARC at the University of Copenhagen. Previously, I was a research fellow in the Division of Mathematical Sciences at Nanyang Technological University hosted by Li, Yi(李翼). I did my PhD at Nanjing University, where I was fortunate to have Professor Yin, Yitong(尹一通) as my adviser.

My research interests lie in the intersection of theoretical computer science and database theory, especially data structure design, data structure complexity, and dimensionality reduction.

Recruitment. I am actively seeking motivated students to join my research group. We offer positions for Master's, PhD, and undergraduate research interns. If you are interested in theoretical computer science, feel free to reach out to me for further discussion.
